Farmer Support Tips & Price Updates

Farmer Support Tips

1. Proper Harvesting Techniques
 Harvest your produce early in the morning to maintain freshness and reduce heat damage.

2. Sorting & Grading
 Sort produce according to size and quality to attract better prices and market trust.

3. Post-Harvest Handling
 Use clean crates instead of sacks to reduce bruising during transportation.

4. Soil Management Tips
 Apply organic manure and rotate crops to maintain soil fertility throughout the year.

5. Pest & Disease Control
 Regularly inspect gardens and use recommended agro-inputs to prevent losses.
