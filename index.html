About Us

Amina Fresh Produce Centre is a leading agro-business in Kayunga Town that supplies a wide range of fresh fruits and vegetables to customers across Uganda. We work closely with local farmers to ensure that all produce reaching our customers is of high quality, well-sorted, and delivered on time. Our centre handles both small and large orders, serving households, market vendors, restaurants, and institutions.

What We Do

·Daily supply of fresh fruits and vegetables

·Linking farmers to reliable buyers

·Providing market price updates

·Offering farmer advisory services

·Supporting value-addition initiatives
